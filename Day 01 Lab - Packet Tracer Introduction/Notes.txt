Lab 01 — Network Devices

Topology:
2x Cisco 2911 Router, 2x Cisco 2960 Switch, 2x ASA 5505 Firewall  
2x PC, 2x Server, 1x Laptop (attacker)  
Connected using Packet Tracer auto-connection type.

Key Takeaways:
Routers connect different networks (Layer 3)
Switches connect devices within a LAN (Layer 2)
Firewalls sit at the network boundary and filter traffic
